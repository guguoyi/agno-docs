## Overview

A workflow that enriches CRM contacts with data from LinkedIn, company websites, and other public sources to improve lead qualification and outreach.

## Planned Features

- LinkedIn profile data extraction
- Company information gathering
- Job title and seniority detection
- Technology stack identification
- Funding and company size data
- Intent signal detection
- CRM field population

## Workflow Steps

```
1. Receive new lead from CRM
2. Search for LinkedIn profile
3. Extract professional details
4. Gather company information
5. Identify buying signals
6. Score lead quality
7. Update CRM record
```

## Use Cases

- Inbound lead qualification
- Account-based marketing
- Sales prospecting
- Customer segmentation
- Target account research
