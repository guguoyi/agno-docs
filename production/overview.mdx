Go from zero to production in three steps: deploy, customize, and connect.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy AgentOS by cloning a template">
    Deploy AgentOS + PostgreSQL to your cloud of choice. Choose Docker, Railway, or AWS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize by adding applications">
    Each application is a complete agent, team, or workflow ready to adapt to your use case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect interfaces for your users">
    Expose your applications via Slack, Discord, MCP, or a custom UI.
  </Step>
</Steps>
